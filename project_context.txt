Assignment: Agentic Research Paper Evaluator
1. Problem Statement
The academic community and independent researchers are often overwhelmed by the sheer volume of papers published on repositories like arXiv. Manually verifying the internal consistency, factual accuracy, and novelty of a paper is time-consuming.
Existing LLM tools often suffer from limited context windows or "hallucination" when summarizing complex technical documents. There is a need for an Agentic AI Platform that doesn't just summarize, but critically audits a research paper by breaking it down into specialized domains of evaluation.
2. Objective
To develop a multi-agent system that autonomously scrapes an arXiv link and executes a comprehensive peer-review simulation. The system must move beyond simple extraction and provide a "Judgement Report" based on cross-referencing internal data and external facts.
3. Scope and Expectations
You are expected to leverage free-tier LLM providers ( Ollama models [ check mac confi   and download the model]) to power the agents.
Special Consideration: Do not send more than 16k tokens in a single LLM call. 
The Platform must execute the following workflow:
Web Scraping: Extracting clean text/data from the provided arXiv URL.
Decomposition: Breaking the paper into sections (Abstract, Methodology, Results, Conclusion).
Agentic Analysis: Utilizing specialized agents for:
Consistency: Checking if the methodology actually supports the claimed results.
Grammar & Language: Evaluating professional tone and syntax.
Novelty: Searching for existing literature to determine uniqueness.
Fact-Checking: Verifying cited constants, formulas, or historical data.
Authenticity (Accuracy Score): Calculating a "Fabrication Probability" based on statistical anomalies or logical leaps.
4. Deliverables
A. Codebase
Source Code: A clean, modular repository (GitHub/GitLab).
Configuration: A README.md explaining how to set up environment variables for the chosen LLM provider.
Framework: Use of an agentic framework ( LangGraph) is highly encouraged.
B. Evaluation Report
A PDF or Markdown file generated by the AI for a "Test Case" paper, containing:
Executive Summary: A pass/fail recommendation.
Detailed Scores:
Consistency Score: (0-100)
Grammar Rating: (High/Medium/Low)
Novelty Index: Qualitative description.
Fact Check Log: List of verified vs. unverified claims.
Accuracy/Fabrication Score: A percentage-based risk assessment.
C. Live Demo
Interface: A simple UI (Streamlit).
Walkthrough: A short video or live presentation demonstrating the input of an arXiv link and the real-time generation of the Judgement Report.
